A Bit About Me

I have been working in a Catholic Secondary College since 2016 in I.T support and am now part-time since starting my teaching degree. I bring a passion for all things design, food, and the industrial arts to TAS teaching. I very much look forward to working in a collaborative, creative, and open environment in my future teaching career.

This ePortfolio will give an insight to my future profession as a Secondary Technologies teacher, my personal philosophy, requirements and beliefs about Technology teaching, and personal self-reflection on myself.
