Materials Technology

Materials Technologies explores the properties of resistance materials and investigate including timber, metals, and textiles in order to develop and create solutions to problems.

Digital Technologies

Since technology is an ever-evolving industry, students need to be aware of current issues in the world and the action technology could take amidst it. Within this knowledge, would also be a familiarity of hardware and the properties of components as well as having a level of competence in programming language.

Engineered Systems

Students learn about the design process involved in creating engineered systems and the purpose of material choices and the impacts they have on the product or tool. Students will learn about the laws of motion, force, and energy and apply them to the relevant topics including mechanisms and simple machines.
